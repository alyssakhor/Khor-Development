My Services

Website Development

Developing websites is the core of my business. Whether it is creating your website through WordPress or HTML/CSS code, I will develop the website that fits all of your needs.

Hosting

Hosting can be taken care of one of two ways - I develop and hand over the website to you or I develop and maintain the website through hosting it. Whenever you have changes you want to me, that would be something I would have to do.

SEO

Making your website be on the top searches on Google is very important when it comes to marketing. Developing the website is so much more than writing code. It also involves knowing what will make your website show in the top searches.

Mobile Capabilities

In a world where our smart phones are the main source of Internet use, it is important for your website to be mobile-friendly. The websites I develop will look great on any laptop, tablet, and mobile device.

The websites I develop are made to be easily navigated as well as intuitive. The websites are meant to be friendly to people of all technological experience levels.

Communication
